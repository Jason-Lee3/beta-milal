About Us

Irvine Milal is one of the branches of Milal Mission, which is a non-profit missionary organization whose main purpose is to evangelize, serve, and enlighten people with disabilities.

Three Major Goals:
  • Evangelism
    We preach the gospel to the handicapped and lead them to Jesus. We bring the people with disabilities to Christ and His love.
  • Service
    We help people with disabilities with the love of Christ. We assist those who have special needs with the love of Christ.
  • Enlightenment
    It changes the perception of people with disabilities by informing the church and the world right away. We bring awareness of the rights of the people with disabilities to the world and church.

We preach the gospel to the disabled so that they can have faith in Jesus Christ (evangelism), provide various help and services to alleviate even a little the weakness and inconvenience that people with disabilities have to experience (volunteer), It trains people to live confidently and confidently in the world, improves the wrong perception of people with disabilities, and guides and enlightens the general public to establish a more equal and horizontal relationship.
